1. Who we are

Bluetooth Mic - Karaoke & PA is published by Lucky Tools on Google Play.

2. Microphone

The app requests microphone permission to pass your voice through to connected speakers (phone speaker or Bluetooth) for karaoke, PA and events. Processing is local and real-time. We do not operate a cloud that receives your audio.

3. On-device data

App preferences (for example Premium cache and disclosure acceptance) stay in private app storage until you clear data or uninstall.

4. Advertising (free tier)

The free version may show banner ads and full-screen ads (about every 90 seconds, including while broadcasting) via Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers (including the advertising ID), IP address and interaction data under Google’s policies and your device ad settings.

We do not sell your voice audio. Prefer no ads? Subscribe to Premium.

5. Purchases

Optional monthly Premium subscription (product ID pamic_premium) removes ads while active. Payments and renewals are handled by Google Play. Cancel anytime in Play subscriptions.

6. Internet access

Used for ads, Play Billing, and optional links you open (privacy, support, Play Store).

7. Children

Not directed at children under 13.

8. Your choices and deletion

  • Uninstall or clear app storage to remove local data.
  • Limit ads via Android advertising ID settings.
  • Cancel Premium in Google Play → Payments & subscriptions.
  • See our data deletion page.

9. Safety disclaimer

Use responsible volume levels. Not a certified professional PA system. You are responsible for compliance with local noise and event rules.
